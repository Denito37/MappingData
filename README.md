# NYC DOT Project

## Mapping cooling structures to NYC streets

**Members:** Dennes Lopez, Matteo Ferrantali, & Leila Moumou

### Purpose
- A GIS web-application that shows the resources NYC has for its streets to combat against overexposure to the sun & what areas in the city require more resources to cool down their roads/sidewalks.
